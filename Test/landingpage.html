About Us

Life in the marine world is something new for all of us. It's different but beautiful, and we at AQUAHUBS Real Estates, aspire to make it more mesmerising.

We aim at providing our HUB owners with not only shelter, but also luxurious living with captivating views to increase their love for the ocean.

Conceptualised in 2203, it has been 2 years of providing happiness to families across the ocean.

We hope to continue our mission, provide the best of locations and projects, at the right budget to our beloved customers.
